*Serves 4*
## Ingredients

### Pantry:
- 1/4 cup green olives, finely chopped (about 7 colossal)
- 2 Tbsp capers
- 1 Tbsp olive oil
- Kosher salt and pepper

### Produce:
- 0.5 lb sugar snap peas, thinly sliced on a diagonal
- 1 small shallot, thinly sliced and rinsed
- 2 Tbsp lemon juice (about ½ lemon)
- 1/4 cup parsley or mint, finely chopped
- Handful of snow pea sprouts (about 40g)

###Dairy: 
- 2 oz. feta cheese, plus more for topping


## Method

1. In a large bowl, combine the sliced sugar snap peas, rinsed shallots, chopped olives, and capers. Toss gently to distribute everything evenly.
2. Add the lemon juice and mix well, making sure the acidity lightly coats all the ingredients. Drizzle in the olive oil and toss again until everything is lightly dressed. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
3. Fold in the chopped parsley and snow pea sprouts, then crumble in the feta and give it one final gentle toss. Taste and adjust seasoning if needed, then top with a little extra feta before serving.
